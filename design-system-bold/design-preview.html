Design Philosophy

TipSharePro's design is bold, not bashful—matching the energy of the logo and the confidence of restaurant professionals who use it. High-contrast colors command attention, while clean layouts ensure clarity for financial data.

Powerful

Bold orange CTAs that demand action. No subtle hints—clear direction.

Fair

Clean data tables and transparent calculations. Every number justified.

Transparent

Lime green success states show positive outcomes clearly. No hidden surprises.

The Bold Palette

Colors drawn directly from the TipSharePro logo—burnt orange for action, lime green for success, navy for trust, and cyan for interactivity.
